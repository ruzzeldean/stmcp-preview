About Us, STMCP

Our History

Star Touring Motorcycle Club Philippines (STMCP) is a Non-Government Organization of motorcycle riders. Its purpose is to organize different activities that promotes camaraderie and help the needy community through charity while we get to enjoy the beauty of our country.

Foundation

The Star Touring Motorcycle Club Philippines (STMCP) was founded by four key individuals, known within the club as the "4 Pillars"â€”Erick Lee, Alo Yap, Ericson Gallito, and Mike Miranda. Their shared passion for motorcycles and camaraderie led them to establish the club, aiming to unite riders under a common brotherhood. On July 5, 2021, STMCP was officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), marking its formal recognition as an organization.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our Mission is to build a community among all riders and make a Big Star Family that have Good Camaraderie among its members. We also promote touring all over the Philippines and Experience the Ride that you'll never forget. STMCP and its Local Chapters also give Charities. We'll support and participate in different motorcycle events and activities to pursue, promote and protect the interests of motorcyclists; to develop better and more enthusiastic relations between the general public. STMCP promotes motorcycle and road safety for the welfare of the entire STMCP family.

Vision

Our long-term goal: Create Members for Life. We will earn our members' loyalty. We will listen to them, anticipate their needs, and act to create value in their eyes. We want to be known for our reliability, flexibility, and responsiveness. Growth, longevity and member satisfaction will naturally follow. We will empower our members in an enriching environment of trust, cooperation and mutual respect.

Our Core Values

S - Sharing. One of the main advocacies of STAR is sharing what we can to the needy. We also promote selfless riders that always think not just of him/herself but also the welfare of the others. Share the road share the load. We will travel with a purpose (Leisure, Charities and Social Activities like tree planting, clean up drive etc.). Time will be well spent. We will also make sure that we will have time to share our weekends with our Families and love ones so we won't have weekly activities. Our activities will be limited to Bi-Weekly to give the members time to save and prepare.
T - Transparency. The club promotes truthfulness to its members and aspirants. This includes financial personal and social activities of the club. STAR and its leadership (P4 or the 4 Pillars) will have its co-equal level of decision making to ensure that a certain individual including the President, the Chairman of the Board, the Board Members and other National Executive Leaders will not promote personal gains and interest in the club.
A - Accountability. We highly promote accountability on what we do. We brag and share things such as projects and activities to promote the Club and its accomplishments. We'll also make sure that official members and aspirants will be held accountable of any wrong doings in the club including all the National, Regional and Local Leaders from top to bottom. Certain penalties like suspension from participating to our official activities up to expulsion from the club will be merited for any member/s and aspiring member/s for any violation of the club's rules and regulations.
R - Respect. It will be our everyday practice. Everyone deserves to be respected. Respect not just to its members and officers but also to our co-riders', co-road users, respect to the nature. Respect to the law of the Land and our club by-laws. Respect is the foundation of greatness.
